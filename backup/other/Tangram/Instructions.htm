Tangrams

In this math interactivity, you will fit seven geometric shapes into one big shape.

  1. Click on any one of the eight outlines displayed in the Select an Outline section to select it.
  2. This will result in an enlarged version of the selected outline being displayed on the white screen and seven geometric shapes being displayed in a section below this white screen.
  3. Select the geometric shapes displayed in this section by clicking them. Drag the selected geometric shapes onto the white screen and try to fit them to form the displayed outline. Just remember that no two shapes can overlap.
  4. You can rotate a shape in any direction by clicking an edge and dragging it.
  5. You can also flip a selected shape by clicking the Flip button.
  6. In case you are stuck, click the Hint button. This will generate lines on the enlarged outline, showing where the individual shapes can be fit.
  7. Click the Reset button to reset the shapes and begin again.
  8. Click the Check button when you have finished modeling the shape.
