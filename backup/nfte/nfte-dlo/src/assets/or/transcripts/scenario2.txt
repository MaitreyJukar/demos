More than 40 million adults in the United States wear contact lenses, and about one-third of them replace their contacts every day.

As they stare at themselves in the mirror, what do they think about?

Perhaps they think about how much money they spend on lenses, cases, and solution, or when they will have time to go to the optometrist to buy more lenses.

Many contact lens wearers are dissatisfied with their purchasing options.

If you have an entrepreneurial mindset, there's an opportunity here.
